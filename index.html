How the Love began?

My first experience with coding was at university, where I created a WordPress website for my e- shop. I really enjoyed the process of making it and then after some time, I started with an online HTML and CSS course and immediately fell in love with it. My next step led me to PHP and from there I decided to do a 3-month immersive course at General Assembly. It gave me a lot of technical skills, a good understanding of the industry, and also group problem-solving experiences. I am looking to join a dynamic team where I can collaborate and grow alongside them. Learning in a highly productive environment where everyone can support each other is my number one priority. I welcome constructive criticism and I will do as much as I can to help the team to achieve our goals.
